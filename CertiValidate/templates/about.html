Behind the Technology

Advanced architecture for verifiable digital credentials.

Custom Blockchain Ledger

Instead of relying on a centralized database that can be altered or tampered with by bad actors, CertiValidate uses a proprietary peer-to-peer Linked List architecture secured by heavily encrypted SHA-256 Hashing algorithms.

  • Immutable storage of document metadata.
  • Cryptographic Linkage ensures historical accuracy.
  • Instant Auditing via localized nodes.

Machine Learning Core

While Blockchain guarantees the document exists, AI ensures the document is actually authentic and not a visually altered photoshop replica.

  • Tesseract OCR extracts all physical text context.
  • TF-IDF Vectorization mathematically graphs text semantics.
  • Cosine Similarity scores against known genuine layouts.
